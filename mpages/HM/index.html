A Heart's Meal

Web Interview Series

Given the word "home" for an assignment, we were asked to explore it however we wanted. My personal interest in home dealt with relational memory and how something like food could bring back memories about our home and even of different homes we may have!

I executed the idea as a web interview series where I would join participants for a meal, help them cook that reminded them of home and then interview them over the meal. Questions covered in the interviews touched on topics such participants' upbringing, culture, memories, and their futures.
