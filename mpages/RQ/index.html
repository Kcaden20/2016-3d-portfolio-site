RISD Quickies

Peer-to-Peer Workshops

RISD Quickies is a forum for student-led, student-taught workshops at the Rhode Island School of Design and Brown University. RISD Quickies was started 6 years ago and it's current iteration has been around for 3 years.

I was co-director with Elizabeth Goodspeed and a team consisting of Sonya Fayzieva & Molly Millette. RISD Quickies 2016 held a record-breaking 45 workshops taught by 36 different instructors and attended by 500 students.
