Fuck Content

Web Native Rereading

One thing that interests me is experimenting with ways of consuming information on digital platform. Making the experience more unique and more tied to the content. For this project, I thought about how navigation controls would work for reading articles.

Using Michael Rock's "Fuck Content" and following his mantra, the website's form and the way his text is laid out across it is my authorship to create a stronger connection between the audience and his essay.

Content Served in a Pan if you know what I mean.
