Bumble

A Mobile Born Web Browser

In our class Computer Utopias, we were asked to look at two old programs; HyperCard and SmallTalk and figure out how they would work in today's smartphone era

Hypercard

My group took on HyperCard as our spirit animal and thought a lot about how the conventions existing on the smartphone are computer conventions. We wanted to look at native mobile creation.

Bumble Demo

Features of Bumble include:

CARD Creation

Hyperlinking

Landscape Features

The final product takes into account all of questions and desires but also is considerate towards the trends and the way products work on the app market (such as an app that's too general and broad is bound to fail).

And as such is marketed towards the LARPING community as a way to create their own fictions.
