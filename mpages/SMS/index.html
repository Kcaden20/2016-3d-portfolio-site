Rethink SMS

Non-Disruptive Messaging

The idea is an interpretation of Tristan Harris' Time Well Spent Manifesto to design interfaces that are more ethical and considerate to a person's needs and life.

Along with this another initiative of the project was to rethink how we can interact with the phone in ways more native and natural which is realized through the swipe based interface.
